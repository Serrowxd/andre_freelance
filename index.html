Hello Brother Knights,

I began the second year of my term as Grand Knight of Council 7430 St-Honoré with the same objective, that is to say, with the mission to bring the brothers knights in the unit, to work constantly as a team and always favor the respect of all.

Our council must continue to live and radiate the fundamental values ​​of our Order: Uni-te, Charity, Fraternity and Patriotism. To achieve this, it is essential to have the support and collaboration of each of you.

I therefore pledge to pursue the objectives and orientations of our Order, which are to value the family and to support our Church and our community at all times. However, this task can not be accomplished without the trust, integrity and honesty ... values ​​that are essential to the success of all our projects.

On behalf of all members of the executive and my staff, we wish you a very good and active Colombian year.

Together, let's build bridges between generations for a better tomorrow!

- Daniel Tremblay, Grand Knight of Council 7430
